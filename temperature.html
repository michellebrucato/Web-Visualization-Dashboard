Latitude vs. Temperature

This plot, comparing city latitude and city temp, shows a relationship that as a city gets closer to the equator (latitude=0), the higher the city temp. Subsequently, cities furthest from the equator showed lower max temps overall. Comparing the location of Norway to Singapore, and their differing climates helps drive home this finding.
