Welcome To The Climate Dashboard!



Analyzing Latitude vs. Climate

This dashboard was created to see if there is a correlation between latitude and climate of a city, relative to its proximity to the equator. The dataset contained over 500 globally diverse cities and was pulled using the OpenWeatherMap API.
